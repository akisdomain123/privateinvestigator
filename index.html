Pre-Marital Background Checks

Before you say yes, verify what you have been told. Our pre-marital checks typically cover:

  • Education – verification of degrees and institutions claimed
  • Job & career – current employment, role and stability
  • Family background – basic family details and reputation
  • Financial red flags – lifestyle versus income, visible debts or concerning patterns (within legal limits)

Lifestyle Verification

Get a realistic picture of day-to-day behaviour and habits:

  • Social circle & regular hangouts
  • Habits such as gambling, alcohol, or other risk factors (where observable)
  • General reputation in neighbourhood or workplace
  • Online and offline behavioural patterns (within lawful boundaries)

Post-Marital Investigations

When you sense that something is not right in your marriage, we help you look for facts instead of assumptions. Typical situations we handle:

  • Suspicion of infidelity or secret relationships
  • Concerns about harassment, abuse, or hidden addiction
  • Suspected financial fraud, hidden assets, or undisclosed loans
We do not use illegal methods like phone tapping or hacking. All assignments are carried out using lawful, discreet techniques and the findings are shared with you in a confidential report.
